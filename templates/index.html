    About Homeshati

    Homeshati is a smart and easy-to-use platform designed to help people find the right house at the right price. It predicts the price of a house based on important details like the location, type of property, number of bedrooms, size, furnishing, and available facilities. Users simply enter information such as the state, city, property type (apartment, villa, or independent house), BHK, furnishing status, size in square feet, public transport accessibility, parking, and security features, and Homeshati gives an estimated price for the property.

    Besides predicting house prices, Homeshati also helps users find properties that fit their budget. Users can set a price range, choose their preferred city, and select the number of bedrooms, and the system will suggest suitable properties within these criteria. This makes it easier for buyers to see only the houses they can afford, saving time and effort.

    The platform is designed to be simple and user-friendly. The forms are easy to fill out, and the results are shown clearly with details about each property, the total number of properties found, and the average price. Homeshati combines data and smart algorithms to give accurate estimates and helpful recommendations, so users can make better decisions while buying a house.
